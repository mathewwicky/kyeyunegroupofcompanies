Development Begins at Capital Valley Business Park in Middletown, PA

By Stewart Companies | January 9, 2024

Exciting news from Stewart Properties’ Capital Valley property in Middletown, PA! Development is underway on two new lots, Lot 3 and Lot 4. Lot 3 will soon become the future home of ABC Supply, featuring a 64,400 SF precast tilt-up warehouse. Kinsley Construction is hard at work on the sitework, while Stewart & Tate Construction (GC) is making great progress on the footers for Lot 3. Meanwhile, Lot 4 is being prepared for a 52,000 SF flex warehouse building, with the tenant still to be determined.
